Energy Efficiency Bulb

Energy efficiency or efficient energy use is aimed at reducing the amount of energy used in the working of products and services. In simpler terms, it means doing more with less. It refers to exhausting every ounce of power out of as little energy as possible as a step towards cleaner energy practices. For instance, replacing an incandescent light bulb with a fluorescent one to get the same amount of illumination can be considered a step towards energy efficiency. The term ‘energy efficient’ can be used both in the micro and the macro sense.


Energy Efficiency Refrigerator

A refrigerator is the one appliance that is constantly working in our kitchens. An energy-efficient one would aid you in making your space greener. Due to their old cooling and lighting systems, refrigerators can be expensive energy hogs. The energy-efficient ones are at least 9 per cent more efficient than those that meet minimum efficiency standards. The star-labelled refrigerators come in all sizes and with all the latest features.

Smart Dishwashers

Similar to dishwashers, washing machines use substantial amounts of energy and water if they work on outdated technology. With an energy-efficient option, you could save up to 40 per cent water and 25 per cent energy. Let’s not forget that these percentages translate to certain amounts of savings that work well for households, businesses and the planet.


Smart Air Conditioners

Energy-efficient air conditioners have an important role to play when it comes to responsible cooling. All of us continue to spend a lot of money cooling our homes. An energy-efficient air conditioner might use 30 to 50 per cent less energy than older, less-efficient units.



Smart Ceiling Fans

Ceiling fans are lifesavers if you live in areas of the country that are humid and hot almost all year round. Though they can’t replace air conditioning, ceiling fans can decrease your need for energy-intensive cooling. Invest in them to save energy and, thus, reduce your expenditure.


Smart LED TV & Monitor

The Energy Saving feature in Smart TV adjusts the brightness of your TV to reduce Power Consumption. It can also help to reduce eye fatigue especially in dim and dark room. This feature is available in Eco Solution option of Smart TV. There are Five settings in Energy Saving feature : Off, Low, Medium, High, Picture Off.

The present invention belongs to a clothes ironing tool. At present, the purpose can be achieved by ironing for many times by an electric iron, the power consumption is high, and the heat inertia is great. The present invention provides a novel electric iron. The clothes can be successfully ironed once, the electricity is saved by 50%, and the heat inertia is little. The present invention has the working principle that an ironing working surface is divided into a cold part and a hot part. The fixing effect of the cold ironing working surface is good, and therefore, the ironing effect and the ironing quality are improved. The hot ironing working surface is used for heating, can be formed into a thin shape, and is provided with a plurality of holes. Besides, the hot ironing working surface can be omitted, the heat capacity is reduced, and therefore, the heat inertia is reduced. The present invention has the advantages of high heat utilization and energy saving.

Advantages

Environmental

Increased efficiency can lower greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions and other pollutants, as well as decrease water use.

Economic
